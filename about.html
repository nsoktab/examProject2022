GOLĘCZEWO 101

Golęczewo is a village of more than 1,370 inhabitants located north of Poznan. Very well developed infrastructure and location makes more and more new people, including Poznanians, want to make Golęczewo their new home.

Golęczewo
Go-LEN-che-vo

In Golęczewo you can find monuments telling the more than 700-year history of the village, including its most interesting part when it was called Golenhofen. It is also a place where close contact with beautiful nature is your daily habit.

Residents can get involved in a number of associations aimed at activating the local community, organizing events or promoting the village and its history.

If you are looking for a balance between enjoying the conveniences of a big city and being surrounded by nature and wildlife on a daily basis, Golęczewo may be the perfect choice.

This site aims to show you those places that make up the identity of the village. It presents places liked by locals that may soon become your favorites as well.
