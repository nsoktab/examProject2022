COMMUNITY

If you want to keep up to date with events important to the village join the groups below or like the Facebook profiles of the associations.

SOŁECTWO GOLĘCZEWO

The village mayor, Mrs Katarzyna Kachel, regularly informs about planned investments or announcements concerning Golęczewo on the village profile.

FOLLOW PAGE

KGW GOLĘCZEWO

Active Golęczewian women have formed Koło Gospodyń Wiejskich and organise events for women and beyond every year. One Valentine's Day card guaranteed for you!

FOLLOW PAGE

GOLĘCZEWIANIE

The "Golęczewianie" Facebook group brings together residents and those willing to become residents. The range of topics is wide.

JOIN GROUP

MUNICIPAL GUARD

If your dog goes for a walk without your knowledge, it may encounter the wardens of the Suchy Las Municipality and then become famous on their Facebook page. Worth observing.

FOLLOW PAGE

GOLENHOFEN

If you want to know more about the history of Golęczewo, the best source of knowledge is the website of Mr. Grzegorz Grupinski, who regularly shares excerpts from articles from decades ago or photos of old Golęczewo.

VISIT PAGE

ZERO WASTE

On the Zero Waste - Municipality of Suchy Las group, the municipality's residents put up items they want to let go into the second circuit free of charge.
