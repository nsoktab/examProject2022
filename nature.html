NATURE PRESERVE

On the border with Zielątkowo, the Natura 2000 protected area "Dolina Samicy" was delimited, running through forest, meadows and farmland along the riverbed.

WILDLIFE

This is the best place to observe wildlife - many species of birds, roe deer, foxes and look for signs of beaver activity. If you enjoy running or cycling this is an excellent way to explore the area.

PS. In autumn, go mushroom picking without limits

IMPORTANT

Just remember that you are a guest there, not an owner. So please keep it quiet and keep your candy wrappers in your pockets.
