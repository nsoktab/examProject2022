CHERRY ORCHARD

The most beautiful walking route is definitely the one around the cherry orchard, which is a charming place at any time of the year.

LITTLE JAPAN

In spring, it is stunning with its white blossoms. Order delicious sushi from Dokku Sushi with free delivery to Golęczewo, have a picnic on the nearby meadow and enjoy the closest Japan experience you can have in Golęczewo.

CHERRIES

During the July-August season, you can buy delicious cherries and peaches straight from the supplier, the Spitalniak family, who have been running the business for three generations now. Follow their Facebook profile Sad Pod Wiatrakiem to know when the season starts and prices.

PS. Ask your neighbours who is the go-to address if you want to buy tomatoes, pears or eggs from happy hens, because in Golęczewo we have several such families.

SUNSET SPOT

Don't be afraid to venture off the road and walk down to the Samica Valley, the best place to admire sunsets in Golęczewo and pick herbs for tea.
